Why Healthcare?

The healthcare industry is a field that involves many different facets. Those who interact with the field are very diverse including the patients, doctors, insurance agents, nurses, pharmacists, the families of patients, students and more. The variety of actors in the field in addition to the high risks associated with security breaches make the domain ideal for creating vignettes. Within the industry the data being protected includes information relating to personal identity, financial data,as well as medicines and treatments for patients. If data such as these were breached it would lower the reputation of the hospital and place patients and their families at risk of not only poor treatment but identity theft. When crafting vignettes keep the idea of healthcare in mind. For articles on security in the healthcare industry please click here or here .
